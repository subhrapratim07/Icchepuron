Belemath Sishu Sikshalay

On October 23rd, 2021, we initiated this project with the children of Belemath. Belemath is a small indigenous village located 3 kilometers from Nahata. The aim of our project is to guide these young boys and girls, helping them continue their education, develop a focus on learning, and grow into responsible individuals who can stand by others in the future.

We visit them Two day in a week,Tuesday and Sunday, to teach and support them in their studies. To ensure they face no difficulties in continuing their education, we provide them with basic study materials like notebooks and pens, within our means. Additionally, to encourage their regular attendance, we offer them some refreshments every Tuesday and Sunday during our sessions.

Education And Awareness Camps

Education and awareness camps focused on fostering a supportive learning environment for children through interactive activities like distributing notebooks, pens, toys, and chocolates to motivate them. We also organized sessions with parents to discuss the importance of education, time management, and healthy lifestyle choices. The aim was to discover hidden talents, emphasize the value of education in daily life, and raise awareness about how parents can contribute to their children's academic and personal development.These camps provided a platform for open dialogue, encouraging both children and parents to invest in education and future success.

Icchepuron Scholarship

Icchepuron has launched the "Icchepuron Scholarship" project to support underprivileged and talented students in Nahata and its surrounding areas. Since July 1, 2021, we have been providing yearly onetime scholarships to selected students. The selection process involves three stages:

  1. First Stage: A written exam is conducted to shortlist students for the scholarship.
  2. Second Stage: We visit the homes of students who pass the written exam to assess their financial condition.
  3. Third Stage: Final interviews are conducted by local school teachers to select the scholarship recipients.
