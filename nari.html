Know Your Rights!!

  • Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition, and Redressal) Act, 2013:

    This act seeks to protect women from sexual harassment at the workplace. It includes language with sexual overtones, invasion of private space, subtle touches, and innuendoes.

  • The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006:

    This act defines child marriage as a marriage where the bride is under 18 years or the groom is under 21. It became effective in 2007 to prevent child marriages.

  • National Commission for Women Act, 1990:

    The NCW represents the rights of women and provides a voice for their concerns. This act aims to improve the status of women and support their economic empowerment.

  • The Immoral Traffic (Prevention) Act, 1956:

    This act seeks to prevent immoral trafficking in pursuance of the International Convention signed in 1950.
