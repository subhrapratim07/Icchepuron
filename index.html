Welcome to Icchepuron Established 2019

Icchepuron, an NGO was founded to walk with aboriginal and to provide adequate light of education to them. It is placed at Nahata Bazar, North 24 Parganas, 743290. Icchepuron association has been starting their journey since 3rd Oct 2019.

At Icchepuron, we are dedicated to enhancing the quality of life for vulnerable and underserved communities. Our mission is to provide holistic support, including healthcare, education, and emotional well-being, to individuals in need. We believe in fostering a compassionate society where every person has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Ambitions

To convince the necessities of education

Education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty, empowering individuals to build better futures for themselves and their communities.

To increase awareness of child marriage

Icchepuron NGO is committed to raising awareness about the dangers of child marriage and advocating for its prevention.

To avoid the superstitions of our society as well as religion

Icchepuron works to eliminate societal and religious superstitions through education and awareness.

To raise awareness about self-health care

Icchepuron NGO strives to enhance self-care awareness and promote healthy living practices among underserved communities.

To uplift marginalized communities effectively

Icchepuron NGO strives to enhance the lives of priority marginalized communities through education and support.

Ensuring women’s safety and empowerment

Icchepuron is committed to ensuring women's safety and empowerment through comprehensive support and advocacy.

Governing Body - Icchepuron
